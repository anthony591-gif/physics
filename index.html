Physics Concepts

🚀 Kinematics

Formula: v = u + at

Definition: Study of motion without considering forces.

Key Variables:

  • v = final velocity
  • u = initial velocity
  • a = acceleration
  • t = time

⚙️ Newton's Laws

Formula: F = ma

Definition: Relationship between force, mass, and acceleration.

Key Variables:

  • F = force (Newtons)
  • m = mass (kg)
  • a = acceleration (m/s²)

⚡ Work & Energy

Formula: W = F × d × cos(θ)

Definition: Energy transfer through force and displacement.

Key Variables:

  • W = work (Joules)
  • F = force
  • d = displacement

🌍 Gravity

Formula: F = G(m₁m₂)/r²

Definition: Attractive force between massive objects.

Key Variables:

  • G = gravitational constant
  • m = masses
  • r = distance

🔊 Waves & Sound

Formula: v = f × λ

Definition: Propagation of disturbances through a medium.

Key Variables:

  • v = velocity
  • f = frequency
  • λ = wavelength

🌡️ Thermodynamics

Formula: Q = mcΔT

Definition: Study of heat and temperature.

Key Variables:

  • Q = heat energy
  • m = mass
  • c = specific heat capacity
  • ΔT = temperature change
